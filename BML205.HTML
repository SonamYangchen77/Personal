Entreprenuership

Welcome to BML205

Entrepreneurship is the process of starting a business, usually a startup, with the goal of making a profit. It involves identifying a business opportunity, conducting market research, assembling a team, and developing a marketing and financing strategy. Entrepreneurship also involves taking risks and making decisions. Entrepreneurship can also refer to the act of transforming the world by solving large-scale problems. Entrepreneurs can create social change by developing products or services that address social issues. Entrepreneurship can be a risky way to make a living, but it can also lead to incredible rewards and lasting success.
