What is Karate?

Karate is a martial art that originated in Japan, focused on hand-to-hand combat and using strikes such as punches, kicks, knee strikes, and elbow strikes. It is known for its strong discipline and philosophy, along with physical fitness and self-defense techniques.

Benefits of Karate

  • Improves physical fitness
  • Enhances discipline and focus
  • Boosts self-confidence
  • Helps in self-defense
